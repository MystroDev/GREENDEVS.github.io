Our Partners

CIPROMED consortium

The CIPROMED consortium comprises 17 partners from ten countries including eight industrial and eight research partners. CIPROMED brings together different experts from Biotechnology, Agriculture, Aquaculture, Food Engineering and Food Production, and reaches societies through broad exploitation of the results, aiming at ensuring the availability of recovered protein sources to cover the nutritional needs of aquaculture, livestock production and human consumption within the Mediterranean Region and beyond.

Target

The Green association for Development and Green Innovations works to achieve the following objectives

Environmental Protection

Contribute to the preservation of the environment and the protection of natural resources and biodiversity against the dangers that could lead to environmental or ecological imbalances given current climate changes.

Sustainable development

Contribute to sustainable development at local, regional and national level in various environmental, agricultural and renewable energy fields through new approaches, using modern, innovative and environmentally friendly technologies.

Cooperation

Coordination and cooperation with various ministries, institutions, associations and national organizations, international, governmental and non-governmental organizations working in the environmental, ecological and agricultural fields, to exchange experiences and carry out joint activities within the framework of cooperation projects that contribute to development environmental policies to protect natural resources and promote agricultural activities.

Research and conferences

Organize forums and seminars, conduct conferences, publish research and periodicals in relation to the objectives of the association, with the help of local and international experts specialized in environmental, agricultural and sustainable development fields, in collaboration and coordination with local, regional, national authorities and international audiovisual media.

Economic development

Contribute to economic, social and environmental development (particularly in the rural area), encourage projects of a structuring nature, seek sources of financing from government and non-governmental organizations national and international governments, and prepare the necessary studies addressing national, regional and international challenges. Be open to local, regional, national and international development experiences and exchange experiences and visits with various national and international governmental and non-governmental organizations.

Awareness and training

Contribute to the awareness and training of people and organizations working in the same field of the association and in relation to its objectives. Contribute to encouraging skills in the fields of environment, ecology, agriculture and development sustainable at local, regional and international levels, contribute to the involvement of young people and give importance to the role of women in sustainable development.

Presentation

P resentation

The Association for Green Development and Innovation (GDI) is an ONG working in the field of preservation of the environment and the protection of natural resources and biodiversity in the face of dangers likely to cause imbalances environmental or ecological, in the current context of climate change. GDI contributes to sustainable development at local levels, regional and national in various environmental, agricultural and renewable energy fields, adopting renewable approaches, modern technologies and innovative and environmentally friendly technologies. Its objective is to contribute to the promotion of economic, social and environmental development (especially in rural areas). It also contributes to raising awareness of issues related to the environment, to ecology, agriculture and sustainable development by involving young people and giving particular importance to role of women in sustainable development.

Our projects

CiproMed Project Challenge Solution for well-being .

Project entitled “Circular and inclusive use of alternative proteins in Mediterranean value chains" (acronym CIPROMED), in which Morocco is a partner, we wish to support all project activities aimed at implementing a circular approach model for the use of alternative protein sources for the agri-food system. If the proposal is funded, we believe the project will contribute greatly to agriculture and sustainable development at the local and regional level.
